ABOUT US

The system " My virtual school " can be used by both teachers and students. It would be in great use for all levels of education in Bulgaria. After you sign in,you would be able to see the interesting modules,which include a forum, an electronic system for homework and many useful resources.

FOR TEACHERS



Teachers would be able to post homework for students. They would also be able to post articles in the forum, which would help the students manage their homework correctly. They would be able to answer the students' questions at any time. After receiving the homework and checking it,they would be able to mark it and send notes.

FOR STUDENTS

There are many opportunities for the students as well. They will be able to follow the schedule with their homework, do it on their computers and then quickly send it electronically to their teacher. The students’ contribution to the blog will be crucial. One of the pages will give them the opportunity to contact other students and discuss questions, occured in the process of executing the task and to share their experience and resources. They can also ask the teachers questions and get an answer. After handing in their homework, the students will receive a mark and remarks on the task.
